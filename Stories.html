Marriage-Based Green Card

Reunited After Years Apart

The Challenge:

"Ana" had been separated from her U.S. citizen husband for 3 years due to a previous visa denial. The couple faced intense scrutiny about the legitimacy of their marriage.

Our Approach:

We meticulously documented their 8-year relationship history, addressed the prior denial concerns, and prepared them for a rigorous interview process.

The Outcome:

Ana received her green card in just 11 months. She's now living with her husband and pursuing her nursing career in California.

"After so many years of uncertainty, Liberty gave us back our future together. They treated our case with such care—like we were family."

PROTECTION GRANTED
Asylum Case

Safety After Persecution

The Challenge:

"James," a journalist from East Africa, faced death threats for exposing government corruption. He entered the U.S. on a visitor visa but feared returning home.

Our Approach:

We gathered extensive documentation of his work and threats, connected him with country condition experts, and prepared a detailed affidavit explaining his credible fear.

The Outcome:

After a 4-hour interview, James was granted asylum. He's now safely resettled with his family and continues his journalism work.

DEPORTATION STOPPED
Cancellation of Removal

A Father's Second Chance

The Challenge:

"Carlos," a construction worker and father of three U.S. citizen children, faced deportation after 18 years in the U.S. due to an old misdemeanor conviction.

Our Approach:

We demonstrated his exceptional community contributions, secured pardons for the old conviction, and proved his deportation would cause exceptional hardship to his disabled daughter.

The Outcome:

The immigration judge granted Cancellation of Removal. Carlos received his green card and can now provide for his family without fear.

"I can't describe what it means to know I won't be taken from my kids. Liberty fought for me when no one else would."

CITIZENSHIP APPROVED
Naturalization

From Green Card to Citizenship

The Challenge:

"Linh," a Vietnamese refugee who came to the U.S. as a child, had been a permanent resident for 20 years but feared applying for citizenship due to past minor offenses.

Our Approach:

We obtained court records showing her offenses qualified as petty exceptions, prepared a detailed moral character packet highlighting her community service, and conducted extensive interview preparation.

The Outcome:

Linh became a U.S. citizen at a moving ceremony surrounded by family. She registered to vote that same day.

VISA GRANTED
O-1 Extraordinary Ability

The Scientist Who Couldn't Wait

The Challenge:

"Dr. Aditya," a renowned cancer researcher from India, needed to begin work at a U.S. university immediately to lead a critical study. Regular processing times would have delayed the research by 6+ months.

Our Approach:

We compiled evidence of his extraordinary ability, including 200+ citations of his work, media coverage, and letters from Nobel laureates. Filed for premium processing and prepared a detailed petition showing urgent national interest.

The Outcome:

O-1 visa approved in just 12 days. Dr. Aditya began his research on schedule, which has since led to breakthrough findings in early cancer detection.

"Liberty understood the stakes of my work. Their strategic approach saved months of delays—time that translated directly into lives potentially saved."

WAIVER APPROVED
I-601 Hardship Waiver

Overcoming Past Mistakes

The Challenge:

"Miguel" was barred from re-entering the U.S. for 10 years due to prior unlawful presence. His U.S. citizen wife struggled with severe depression and relied on him as her primary caregiver.

Our Approach:

We worked with medical professionals to document the extreme hardship his wife would face, demonstrated his rehabilitation, and built a compelling case showing his absence would create exceptional and extremely unusual hardship.

The Outcome:

Waiver approved in 8 months. Miguel reunited with his wife and is now adjusting status to become a permanent resident.
