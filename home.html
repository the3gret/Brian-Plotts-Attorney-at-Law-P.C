Our Immigration Services

Comprehensive legal solutions designed to meet your specific immigration needs and help you achieve your goals.

Family-Based Immigration

Reunite families with expert guidance on spouse visas, fiance visas, and family sponsorship applications.

Services Include:

  • Spouse visas (CR-1/IR-1)
  • Fianc√©(e) visas (K-1)
  • Parent sponsorship
  • Sibling sponsorship
  • Adjustment of status
  • Consular processing

Process Overview:

Our family-based immigration process begins with a thorough consultation to assess your eligibility. We'll guide you through petition preparation, document collection, and application submission. For consular processing cases, we prepare clients for interviews and handle all communications with the National Visa Center.

Requirements:

  • Proof of bona fide relationship
  • Financial support documentation (I-864)
  • Medical examination results
  • Police clearance certificates
  • Completed application forms

Work Visas

H-1B, L-1, TN, E-2 and other employment-based visa solutions for professionals and businesses.

Visa Types We Handle:

  • H-1B (Specialty Occupations)
  • L-1 (Intracompany Transferees)
  • TN (NAFTA Professionals)
  • E-1/E-2 (Treaty Traders/Investors)
  • O-1 (Extraordinary Ability)
  • P (Athletes/Entertainers)
  • R (Religious Workers)

Our Approach:

We work closely with both employers and employees to secure the appropriate work authorization. Our team prepares comprehensive petition packages, including labor condition applications (LCAs) when required, and provides strategic guidance for cap-subject visas like the H-1B.

Processing Times:

Standard processing typically takes 3-6 months, but we can expedite certain cases through Premium Processing (15 calendar days for additional fee). We'll advise you on the best strategy based on your timeline and needs.

Green Cards

Permanent residency applications through employment, family, investment, or special programs.

Green Card Pathways:

  • Family-sponsored (immediate relatives & preference categories)
  • Employment-based (EB-1 to EB-5)
  • Diversity Visa Lottery
  • Asylum/Refugee status
  • Special immigrant categories
  • VAWA self-petitioners

Our Services:

We handle all aspects of the green card process, from initial eligibility assessment to final approval. This includes preparing and filing Form I-485 (Adjustment of Status) or consular processing, responding to Requests for Evidence (RFEs), and preparing clients for interviews.

Timeline & Fees:

Processing times vary significantly by category (6 months to several years). Government filing fees range from $1,140 to $3,675 depending on applicant age and category. We offer fixed-fee packages for most green card applications.

Citizenship & Naturalization

Guidance through the naturalization process to help you become a U.S. citizen.

Eligibility Requirements:

  • At least 18 years old
  • Permanent resident for 3-5 years
  • Continuous residence in U.S.
  • Physical presence requirement
  • Good moral character
  • English language proficiency
  • Knowledge of U.S. history and government

Our Naturalization Process:

We guide clients through every step of the naturalization process, from determining eligibility to preparing Form N-400. Our services include reviewing your immigration history for potential issues, preparing you for the civics test and English interview, and representing you at your naturalization ceremony.

Benefits of Citizenship:

U.S. citizens can vote, petition for more family members, obtain citizenship for children born abroad, travel with a U.S. passport, access more government jobs, and never worry about losing status or being deported.

Deportation Defense

Aggressive representation in removal proceedings and appeals.

Defense Strategies:

  • Cancellation of Removal
  • Asylum and Withholding of Removal
  • Adjustment of Status in Proceedings
  • VAWA Cancellation
  • U Visa for Crime Victims
  • T Visa for Trafficking Victims
  • SIJS for Abandoned Juveniles

Our Court Representation:

We provide full representation in immigration court, from master calendar hearings to individual hearings. Our attorneys are experienced in cross-examining DHS witnesses, presenting documentary evidence, and making legal arguments before immigration judges.

Emergency Situations:

If you or a loved one has been detained by ICE, we can file for bond redetermination, seek stays of removal, and pursue habeas corpus petitions when appropriate. Time is critical in detention cases - contact us immediately for assistance.

Business Immigration

Corporate immigration solutions including EB-5 investor visas and multinational executive transfers.

Business Immigration Services:

  • EB-5 Investor Visas ($900k/$1.8M investments)
  • L-1A/B Intracompany Transferees
  • E-1/E-2 Treaty Trader/Investor Visas
  • PERM Labor Certification
  • Multinational Manager/Executive Petitions
  • TN Visas for Canadian/Mexican Professionals
  • H-1B for Specialty Occupation Workers

Our Corporate Approach:

We work closely with HR departments and corporate counsel to develop immigration strategies that align with business objectives. Our services include compliance audits, I-9 verification training, and customized immigration policies for companies with foreign national employees.

Investment Immigration:

For EB-5 investors, we provide end-to-end services including regional center evaluation, source of funds documentation, I-526 and I-829 petition preparation, and conditional permanent residence issues. We also assist with direct EB-5 investments and job creation calculations.
