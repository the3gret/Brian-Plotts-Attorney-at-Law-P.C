Our Mission

At Brian Plotts Attorney at Law P.C., we believe every family deserves the opportunity to build a secure future in the United States. Our mission is to provide exceptional legal representation that transforms lives while upholding the highest standards of integrity and professionalism.

We navigate the complex immigration system so our clients can focus on what matters most—their families, careers, and dreams for a better life.

"Immigration is not just about laws—it's about people. We're honored to be part of our clients' journeys to security and belonging."

— Maria Rodriguez, Founding Partner

Our Founding Story

Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2005 by Maria Rodriguez, Horizon began as a solo practice serving immigrant families in New York City. Maria's own experience as a child of immigrants inspired her to dedicate her career to immigration law.

Growing to Serve

By 2012, demand for our compassionate approach led to expanding our team. Today, we're a diverse group of 12 attorneys and 15 support staff serving clients nationwide.

Recognized Excellence

With over 5,000 successful cases, we've been recognized by the American Immigration Lawyers Association for our commitment to excellence and pro bono service.

Maria Rodriguez, Esq.

Founding Partner

A graduate of Columbia Law School, Maria has dedicated her 25-year career exclusively to immigration law. Fluent in Spanish and Portuguese, she specializes in complex deportation defense and family reunification cases.

Maria serves on the AILA National Pro Bono Committee and has testified before Congress on immigration policy reform.
